Who We Are

DRIVEN BY TRUST. BUILT ON RESULTS.

Zaki Automobile is a premier automobile dealership based in Kano State, Nigeria. We specialize in importing, bidding, buying, and selling quality vehicles to individuals and businesses across the region.

Since our founding, we have maintained a simple promise: honest transactions, quality vehicles, and customer satisfaction above everything else. Every car we list is personally inspected and verified before it reaches our customers.

Whether you're looking to buy your first car, upgrade to something premium, or import a specific model from abroad — Zaki Automobile is the partner you can trust.
